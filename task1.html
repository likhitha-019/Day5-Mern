FIFA World Cup: The Global Football Spectacle The FIFA World Cup is the most prestigious international football tournament, held every four years and watched by millions of fans around the globe. Organized by the Fédération Internationale de Football Association (FIFA), the tournament brings together national teams from across continents to compete for the ultimate prize in football. First held in 1930, the World Cup has grown into a celebration of culture, unity, and sporting excellence, with countries like Brazil, Germany, and France among the most successful participants. Beyond just sport, the event has a profound impact on global tourism, the economy, and international relations.
